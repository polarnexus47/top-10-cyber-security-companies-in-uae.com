About This Ranking

Independent research helping security decision-makers in the UAE find the right cybersecurity partner.

Who We Are

We are a team of cybersecurity researchers and security procurement analysts with experience evaluating vendors across the MENA region. Our backgrounds span offensive security, compliance advisory, and enterprise security procurement. We do not represent any vendor on this list, and no company has paid for placement or had advance knowledge of their ranking position.

What This Site Is

An editorial ranking resource — not a vendor marketplace, not a lead generation platform for any single company, and not an aggregator of paid reviews. Every company profile is written by our editorial team based on research, not submitted by the company itself.

This site publishes an independent ranking of cybersecurity companies active in the UAE market. We are focused on helping CISOs, IT managers, and procurement teams cut through vendor marketing and find providers that actually match their requirements.

What This Site Is Not

This site does not accept payment for rankings, sponsored placements, or "featured" positions. If a company has contacted us requesting paid placement, we have declined. Our Editorial Policy documents this in full.

Coverage Scope

We cover cybersecurity service providers — penetration testing firms, managed security service providers (MSSPs), compliance consultancies, and security technology vendors — that are either headquartered in the UAE or maintain a substantial operational presence serving UAE enterprise clients.

Coverage is not exhaustive. The ranking lists ten companies because a ten-position list is useful for buyers comparing shortlists. We do not list companies we cannot independently verify. Companies can submit for consideration via our submission form.

Update Schedule

Rankings are reviewed quarterly. Major market events — acquisitions, accreditation changes, significant incidents — trigger immediate review of affected company profiles. The main page always displays the last-updated date prominently.

Contact

Questions, corrections, or requests for inclusion can be submitted via our contact page or submission form.
